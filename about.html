About us

We began operations in 1998 under the name SK Choudhary Civil Contractor & Labour Supplier As of now, our ancillary service has attracted more than five units in the Pune and Mumbai Industrial Belt. We currently employ more than 100 Labours (skilled and unskilled), and we have accomplished a great deal over the years. Our mission has always been to create a world where our skilled workforce can assist in minimizing the timeline for your project.


SK Choudhary creates and delivers high-impact solutions that enable our clients to achieve their business goals and enhance their competitiveness. With over 24 years of experience, company creates unique time to value through a comprehensive suite of innovative solutions that help clients win in the Human Age.

These solutions cover an entire range of talent-driven needs from recruitment and assessment, training and development, and career management, to outsourcing and workforce consulting. Small and medium-sized enterprises, local, multinational, and global companies. We are in ascending mode. We have a systematic approach to supplying uninterrupted skilled, semi-skilled, and unskilled labours. We have a large data bank of labours wherein we can cater to any requirements of our clients.

For the last 24 years, we provide sufficient and necessary qualified staff to our clients. includes manufacturing units, electrical units & all kinds of Construction works, etc. We have professionally qualified staff and supervisors to cater needs of our customers. We believe in nurturing the relationship. We are equipped with the latest computerized system to serve customers effectively & quickly and maintain the records.
